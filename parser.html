Parser DDSL¶

Fan’s parser DDSL is fully self-explainable, its runtime has enough meta-data to reconstruct itself.

  • Learn the syntax by yourself

    Suppose you have toplevel installed successfully (see Toplevel support).

    $ #directory "+fan";;
$ #load "fanTop.cma";;
