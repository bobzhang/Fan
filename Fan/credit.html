Fan is a successor to Camlp4, which was mainly developed by Daniel de Rauglaudre and Michel Mauny, and later was largely renovated by Nicolas Pouillard.

In fall semester 2011, I had an indepedent study with Stephanie on "generic programming in Haskell", later I ported it into OCaml using Camlp4 in spring semester 2012.

Shortly, I was annoyed by some designs of Camlp4, and started Fan as a research project under the guidance of Steve, from July 2012.
